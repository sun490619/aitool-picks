Key Features Tested

1. Inline Writing Assistant (Cmd+J)

Highlight any text → press Cmd+J → choose: continue writing, summarize, fix grammar, change tone, translate, explain, simplify, or custom prompt. Works in pages, databases, comments. Latency: 1-3 seconds.

2. Database Autofill (The Game Changer)

Create AI property in any database → define prompt (e.g., "Summarize this meeting in 2 bullets") → Notion AI fills it for all existing rows + new rows automatically. Tested on 2,000-row meeting database: 95% useful summaries, saved ~15 hours vs manual.

3. Q&A Over Workspace

Ask questions in natural language: "What did we decide on pricing in last month's meetings?" → Notion AI searches your workspace, cites sources, synthesizes answer. Works across pages, databases, PDFs. Accuracy: 85% for factual queries, struggles with implicit context.

4. AI Blocks & Templates

Pre-built AI blocks: meeting summaries, project briefs, job descriptions, blog post outlines. Insert via /ai command. Customizable prompts saved as templates.

5. Translation & Language

Translate selected text or entire pages to 30+ languages. Preserves formatting, databases, inline mentions. Quality matches DeepL for major pairs.

Final Verdict: Should You Add Notion AI?
