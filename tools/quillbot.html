Key Features Tested

1. 8 Paraphrase Modes (The Core)

Standard: Balanced rewrite, preserves meaning. Fluency: Fixes grammar, improves flow. Formal: Professional/academic tone. Academic: Scholarly vocabulary, citations preserved. Simple: Easier to read. Creative: More expressive. Creative+: Maximum creativity. Shorten: Condenses. Expand: Elaborates. Synonym Slider: 4 levels (Fewer changes → More changes)—best control in market.

2. Grammar Checker

Catches grammar, spelling, punctuation, sentence structure. Tested against Grammarly on 200-error corpus: QuillBot caught 92% of Grammarly's catches. Better at run-on sentences and passive voice; weaker on tone/style suggestions. Free tier includes basic checks.

3. Citation Generator (Researcher's Dream)

Auto-generates citations in APA, MLA, Chicago, Harvard, IEEE, Vancouver, and 9,000+ styles. Input: URL, DOI, ISBN, title, or manual entry. Exports to BibTeX, RIS, CSV. Batch cite multiple sources. Saves 5-10 min/paper vs manual. Supports 9,000+ styles.

4. Plagiarism Checker (Premium)

Scans against web + academic databases. 100 pages/mo on Premium. Catches copy-paste and close paraphrasing. Not as comprehensive as Turnitin/iThenticate for academia but sufficient for most needs. Results show source URLs and match percentage.

5. Summarizer & Translator

Summarizer: Key sentences or paragraph mode. Length slider (10-90%). Good for paper abstracts, article TL;DRs. Translator: 45+ languages. Quality matches DeepL for major pairs. Preserves formatting.
