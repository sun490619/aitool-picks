Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: January 1, 2026
Transparency Notice: AI Tool Picks participates in affiliate marketing programs. This page explains how it works and what it means for you.

What Are Affiliate Links?

Some links on this site are affiliate links. When you click an affiliate link and make a purchase, we may earn a commission from the vendor—at no additional cost to you. The price you pay is exactly the same whether you use our link or go directly to the vendor's website.

Why We Use Affiliate Links

Running a review site takes significant time and resources: researching tools, hands-on testing, writing detailed comparisons, maintaining the site, and keeping content updated. Affiliate commissions help cover these costs and allow us to keep publishing high-quality, independent reviews without charging you for access.

Our Commitment to Independence

Commissions do not influence our reviews or recommendations. Our review process is:

  • Hands-on testing: We actually use each tool for real work before reviewing.
  • Criteria-based: We evaluate on features, usability, performance, value, and support—not commission rates.
  • Honest verdict: We publish pros, cons, and skip/worthy verdicts even for high-commission tools.
  • No vendor influence: Vendors do not review, approve, or edit our content before publication.
