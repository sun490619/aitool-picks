1. CapCut (Free) — Best for Short-Form Video Editing

CapCut's free plan includes auto-captions, text-to-speech, and background removal. It's the best free editor for YouTube Shorts, TikTok, and Reels.

2. Canva (Free) — Best for Thumbnails & Graphics

Canva's AI Magic Design generates thumbnails, social posts, and channel art from a text prompt. The free library is enough for most creators.

3. ElevenLabs (Free tier) — Best for AI Voiceovers

ElevenLabs' free tier gives you 3 custom voices and 10 minutes of generation per month. Good enough for voiceovers, narrations, and podcast intros.

4. Descript (Free) — Best for Podcast Editing

Descript lets you edit audio and video by editing text. Remove filler words, clone your voice, and publish to major platforms — all in the free tier.

5. ChatGPT (Free) — Best for Scripts & Outlines

ChatGPT generates video scripts, blog post outlines, and social media captions. The free model handles most content outlining tasks well.

6. Otter.ai (Free) — Best for Transcription

Otter's free tier transcribes 300 minutes per month. Use it to turn podcasts, interviews, and meetings into searchable text.

7. Hootsuite (Free) — Best for Scheduling

Hootsuite's free plan connects 3 social accounts and lets you schedule posts in advance. Good for creators managing multiple platforms.
