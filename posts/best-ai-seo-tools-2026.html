1. Surfer SEO — Best Overall for Serious SEOs

From $99/mo (Essential) · ★★★★★

Surfer pioneered the "Content Score" — a real-time 0-100 score that shows how well your content matches the top-ranking pages for your target keyword. It analyzes 500+ on-page signals including NLP terms, heading structure, word count, and image usage.

Standout features: AI Tracker monitors your brand's visibility in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. The Google Docs integration means you never leave your writing flow.

Who it's for: Agencies and teams producing 20+ articles/month. The $99/mo price is steep for solo users, but the data quality justifies it at scale.

Affiliate tip: 30% commission with a 60-day cookie window — one of the longest in the industry. A single referral on the $219/mo Scale plan earns you ~$66.

From $45/mo (Basic) · ★★★★☆

Frase does 80% of what Surfer does at less than half the price. The automated content briefs save hours — just enter a keyword, and Frase pulls SERP data, competitor outlines, and suggested headings into a ready-to-use brief.

Where it shines: The AI writing assistant generates full sections within your content brief. Content scoring helps you optimize for specific keywords in real time.
