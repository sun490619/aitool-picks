1. Surfer SEO — Best Overall for Serious SEOs

From $99/mo (Essential) · ★★★★★

Surfer pioneered the "Content Score" — a real-time 0-100 score that shows how well your content matches the top-ranking pages for your target keyword. It analyzes 500+ on-page signals including NLP terms, heading structure, word count, and image usage.

Standout features: AI Tracker monitors your brand's visibility in ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Google AI Overviews. The Google Docs integration means you never leave your writing flow.

Who it's for: Agencies and teams producing 20+ articles/month. The $99/mo price is steep for solo users, but the data quality justifies it at scale.

Affiliate tip: 30% commission with a 60-day cookie window — one of the longest in the industry. A single referral on the $219/mo Scale plan earns you ~$66.

2. Frase — Best Value for Solo Creators

From $45/mo (Basic) · ★★★★☆

Frase does 80% of what Surfer does at less than half the price. The automated content briefs save hours — just enter a keyword, and Frase pulls SERP data, competitor outlines, and suggested headings into a ready-to-use brief.

Where it shines: The AI writing assistant generates full sections within your content brief. Content scoring helps you optimize for specific keywords in real time.

Limitations: No AI search visibility tracking (Surfer's key differentiator). The keyword difficulty analysis is weaker than Surfer's.

Affiliate tip: 30% recurring for 12 months. Better recurring income than Surfer's one-time commission if your referrals stick around.

Try Frase →

3. NeuronWriter — Budget King

From $19/mo (Bronze) · ★★★★☆

At $19/mo, NeuronWriter is the cheapest serious AI SEO tool on the market. It combines Surfer-style NLP optimization with Clearscope-quality recommendations at a fraction of the cost.

Best feature: Internal linking suggestions — it analyzes your existing content and recommends which pages to link together for maximum SEO impact. No other tool at this price point does this well.

Affiliate tip: 30% lifetime recurring — the best affiliate deal in SEO tools. A $19/mo Bronze subscriber earns you ~$6/month, potentially for years.

Try NeuronWriter →

4. MarketMuse — Enterprise Content Intelligence

From $149/mo · ★★★★☆

MarketMuse goes beyond content optimization into full content strategy. It analyzes your entire site, identifies content gaps, and prioritizes what to write next based on which topics will move the needle on your domain authority.

Best for: Large content teams at enterprises, media companies, and serious content-driven businesses. If you manage 100+ articles, this pays for itself.

Not for: Solo creators or small businesses — overkill and overpriced for under 50 articles/month.

Try MarketMuse →

5. Scalenut — AI-First Content Creation

From $39/mo (Growth) · ★★★★☆

Scalenut positions itself as an "AI-first" SEO platform — meaning the AI doesn't just guide you, it generates the content. The Cruise Mode feature produces a complete 2000+ word article with SERP research, NLP optimization, and an outline in about 5 minutes.

Best for: Content teams that want AI to do the heavy lifting. If you prefer editing over writing from scratch, Scalenut's workflow is the most "hands-off" of the five.

Try Scalenut →

Our Recommendation

If you're a solo creator on a budget: Frase ($45/mo) or NeuronWriter ($19/mo). Both give you professional-grade optimization without the enterprise price tag.

If you run an agency or content team: Surfer SEO ($99/mo). The AI Tracker, Content Score, and Google Docs integration are worth the premium when content is your business.

If you want the AI to do 90% of the work: Scalenut ($39/mo). Cruise Mode is genuinely impressive for churning out first drafts.
