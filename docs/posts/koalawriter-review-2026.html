KoalaWriter scrapes live SERPs for your target keyword, extracts entities, headings, word counts, and LSI terms from top 10 results, then builds an outline. This is not keyword stuffing—it's semantic optimization based on what's actually ranking now.

2. Auto Internal Linking

KoalaWriter crawls your existing WordPress site, maps content clusters, and inserts contextual internal links during generation. 3. One-Click WordPress Publishing
